Projects

FTC Shooter V1 — Double Flywheel Prototype
Flywheel • CAD • Prototyping • Iteration

Shooter V1 was our first double vertical flywheel shooter. It used two independently driven 6000 RPM motors and lightweight 4-inch wheels. While it achieved high rotational speed and reduced backspin, it suffered from accuracy issues, slow recovery between shots, and no angle adjustment.

FTC Shooter V2 — Adjustable Hood & Counter‑Roller
Hooded Shooter • Counter‑Roller • CAD

Shooter V2 introduced an adjustable hood and counter‑roller system. This version improved shot flexibility and reduced backspin, but friction in the hood joints caused slow rebound times, exposing the need for thrust bearings and belt optimization in V3.
